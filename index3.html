Kids to be Educated as our Goal

To instill a sustainability mindset in children from a young age to save the planet

Sustainability is the cornerstone of a thriving future, and educating children about it from their elementary school days is crucial. By instilling the principles of environmental stewardship early, we nurture a generation inherently equipped to value and protect our natural resources. Understanding sustainability helps children see the impact of their actions on the planet, encouraging responsible behavior and a lifelong commitment to sustainable practices. It prepares them to face and solve the environmental challenges of tomorrow, ensuring a healthier planet for future generations.

Basic Premise

01

Basic Premise

  • The game can be played by 2 players up to 6 players

  • The objective is to make 3 colored sets and maximize the total points per player

  • Each card contains 2 values, a orange number representing bucks/money, and a green number representing green bucks; Orange bucks values need to be multiplied by 4 and green bucks by 6

02

Game Setup

  • Deal 5 cards for 2-4 players; Deal 4 cards for 5-6 players; Lay down the remaining cards so it becomes the draw pile

  • Players go in order taking turns to draw a card from the draw pile and trying to form colored sets; they discard an unwanted card from their hand into a discard pile

  • 3 cards of same color forms a set

03

Set Creation

  • Once players form a set of 3 same colored cards, they put it down face down in front of them in to their bank; Only one set can be formed per play

  • After making a set, player should draw 3 new cards from the pile so they always have same number of cards they started with in their hand

  • Once the draw pile is exhausted, shuffle the discard pile and make that the draw pile

04

Score Tally

  • Game ends when ALL players have got 3 colored sets in their bank

  • Calculate total score by player by multiplying orange points by 4 and green points by 6 and adding it all together. Negative green points need to be added as negative numbers

  • Player with highest total score wins
